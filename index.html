Frequently Asked Questions

How does StreamSync AI learn my style?
Our Style DNA feature analyzes your top-performing content, identifying patterns in timing, transitions, energy levels, and audience engagement points. The AI then applies these insights to create clips that match your unique voice and style.
What types of content work best?
StreamSync AI excels with gaming streams, podcasts, educational content, business coaching, fitness videos, and entertainment shows. It works with any long-form video content where there are distinct moments or highlights to extract.
How long does processing take?
Most videos process in 10-30 minutes depending on length. A 2-hour stream typically takes 15 minutes to analyze and generate clips. You'll receive an email when your clips are ready for download.
Can I edit the AI-generated clips?
Absolutely! While our clips are ready to post, you can fine-tune timing, add custom branding, adjust captions, or modify any element using our built-in editor or export to your preferred editing software.
What's included in the free trial?
Your 7-day free trial includes full access to Pro Creator features: 25 clips, custom AI training, HD exports, and priority support. No credit card required to start.
